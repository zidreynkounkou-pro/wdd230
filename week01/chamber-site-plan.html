Brazzaville Chamber of Commerce

Purpose:

Brazzaville Chamber of Commerce is a small community that gathers most of the organizations that operate in the city of Brazzaville. We help those companies to advertize their products, find potential employees and expand their organization throughout the country through different approches, such as fairs, visuo-conferences ect.

Scenarios:

Economic Development and Investment
Business Expansion and Relocation
Community Engagement and Sponsorship Opportunities
Workforce Development and Training
